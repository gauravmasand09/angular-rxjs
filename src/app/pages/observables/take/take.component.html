Tap

Used to perform side-effects for notifications from the source observable. The most common use of tap is actually for debugging. You can place a tap(console.log) anywhere in your observable pipe, log out the notifications as they are emitted by the source returned by the previous operation. Note: The best use-case for tap() operator is to perform loggin operations because tap, will show everything after subscribe unlike map operator. Basically it cant change before subscribe.
